History of Surfing

Background of Surfing

Surfing began when the first Chinese immigrants tried to come to Hawaii but didn't want to pay the fee to board the boats. They repurposed their Chinese take-out food containers and used glue and duct tape to hold them together, creating the first surfboards. The first issue they encountered was generating a wave big enough to send them all the way to Hawaii. For this, they solved by using your mom.
